# Advanced Analytics Summary

1. Historical VaR and CVaR were calculated for all mutual funds to estimate downside risk. Some funds showed significantly higher tail-risk compared to others.

2. Rolling 90-Day Sharpe Ratio analysis highlighted changes in risk-adjusted performance over time and identified periods of strong and weak fund performance.

3. Investor Cohort Analysis showed that the 2024 investor cohort contributed the majority of total investments and investor participation.

4. SIP Continuity Analysis identified multiple investors with average SIP gaps greater than 35 days, classifying them as "At Risk" for SIP discontinuation.

5. Fund Recommendation Engine successfully recommended top-performing funds based on investor risk appetite (Low, Moderate, High) using Sharpe Ratio and risk classification.

6. Sector Concentration Analysis using the Herfindahl-Hirschman Index (HHI) showed that most funds were reasonably diversified, while a few exhibited moderate concentration risk.
